About SWOT Consultancy

SWOT Consultancy Services, founded by Mr. Sandeep Arora, is a leading provider of Bad Debt Management, Recovery Solutions, and Skip Tracing Services with a legacy spanning over 22 years. Since 1998, we have been a trusted partner for top financial institutions, delivering high-performance recovery strategies backed by compliance, expertise, and innovation.

Expanding our global footprint, Mr. Arora established SWOT Services FZ LLC (U.A.E.), a fully licensed company operating under UAE regulations. The UAE entity specializes in professional debt collection, skip tracing, and marketing support, serving clients with internationally benchmarked processes and operational excellence.
